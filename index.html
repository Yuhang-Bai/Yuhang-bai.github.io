PhD Candidate · Northwestern Polytechnical University

I work in graph theory and theoretical computer science, with interests in approximation algorithms, matroid theory, and random graph theory. My advisor is Professor Shenggui Zhang. From 2024 to 2026, I visited the MTA-ELTE Matroid Optimization Research Group, hosted by Kristóf Bérczi.

Abstract
